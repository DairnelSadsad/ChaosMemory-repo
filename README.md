# Chaos Memory

## Project Descrpition 

Chaos Memory is a fast-paced memory matching card game with a chaotic twist. 
Players flip cards to find matching pairs while managing lives and earning points. 
Unlike traditional memory games, special cards add unpredictable effects: 
Peek cards briefly reveal hidden cards, Heart cards restore lives, and Tornado cards rotate the board to confuse players. 
Each level begins with a short preview of all cards, challenging players to memorize positions before the game starts. 
As difficulty increases, more cards and pairs are added, making the game more intense and strategic.

## Game Instructions

To play Chaos Memory, start by selecting a difficulty level. At the beginning of each round, all cards are briefly revealed for 5 seconds, giving you a chance to memorize their positions before they are hidden again. Your goal is to find and match pairs of normal cards by clicking two cards each turn. If the two cards match, you earn points and the pair stays revealed. If they do not match, you lose a life. Be careful, because some cards are special cards instead of matching pairs. Clicking a Peek card temporarily reveals hidden cards for 3 seconds, a Heart card restores lives, and a Tornado card rotates the board to make memorization harder. When a special card is activated, its effect happens immediately and your turn ends. Continue matching pairs while managing your remaining lives until all possible pairs are cleared or you run out of lives.
